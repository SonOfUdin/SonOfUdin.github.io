A FESTSCHRIFT IN HONOUR OF PROF. O. A. EMEJULU

About the Book

This book is a collection of well-researched scholarly essays in honour of Professor Obiajulu A. Emejelu, both to mark his end of tenure as the fourth substantive Executive Director of the National Institute Nigerian Languages, Aba, Nigeria and to celebrate his contributions to diverse fields of knowledge. The book covers diverse but contiguous fields of studies, including articles in linguistics, literature, information and communication studies, translation studies, and education. There is also an article couched to highlight Emejulu's overwhelming contributions to the National Institute for Nigerian Languages, Aba, and a Post-Script in which the ebullient celebrant shares a NINLAN story.

The book was editted by Dr Famous Oghoghophia IMU, a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Linguistics, National Institute for Nigerian Languages, Aba; Dr. Philip Oghenesuowho EKIUGBO, a faculty member in the Department of Linguistics, National Institute for Nigerian Languages, Aba; and Prof. Asabe Usman KABIR (PhD), a Professor of Oral and African Literatures in the Department of English and Literary Studies, Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto.
